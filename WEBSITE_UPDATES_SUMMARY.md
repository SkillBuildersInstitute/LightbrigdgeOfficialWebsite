## üéØ EASY PRICE UPDATES

All pricing elements now have `data-price` attributes. To update prices:

1. **Service Cards** (in services section):
   - Search for `data-price="service-name"` in service cards
   - Update the price text within that div

2. **Pricing Section**:
   - Search for `data-price="service-name-pricing"` in pricing cards
   - Update the price text within that div

**Example:** To update Custom Solutions pricing:
- Service card: Find `<div data-price="custom-solutions">$5K - $25K</div>`
- Pricing section: Find `<div data-price="custom-solutions-pricing">$5K - $25K</div>`
